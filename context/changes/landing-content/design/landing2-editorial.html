Questions, answered

Is my photo uploaded anywhere? ▲
With the Local engine — never; everything happens in your browser. With Cloud AI the photo is uploaded over an encrypted connection, processed, and the source file is automatically deleted within 24 hours.
Why does Cloud AI need an account? ▼
Cloud processing costs real GPU time. A free account plus a shared daily limit keeps it free for everyone.
Why did my first cloud photo take a few minutes? ▼
After a quiet period the AI model boots from cold — the first run can take a few minutes; the next ones finish in seconds.
