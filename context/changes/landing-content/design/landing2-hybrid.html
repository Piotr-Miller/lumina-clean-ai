Engines

Local vs Cloud AI

Local brightens instantly in your browser — nothing is uploaded. Cloud AI sends the photo to a night-photo model for a visibly cleaner result (free account required).

Controls

Sliders + Auto

Auto reads your photo and sets the sliders; drag any of them to take over. Details live right at the control:

Brightness (gamma) Tooltip · on hover / focusLifts shadows without clipping highlights. Auto picks a starting value from your photo's histogram.
Cost

What's free?

Local is free and unlimited. Cloud AI is free within a small daily community limit — sliders never trigger processing; only the explicit process button does.

FAQ

Is my photo uploaded anywhere? ▲
With the Local engine — never; everything happens in your browser. With Cloud AI the photo is uploaded over an encrypted connection, processed, and the source file is automatically deleted within 24 hours.
Why does Cloud AI need an account? ▼
Cloud processing costs real GPU time. A free account plus a shared daily limit keeps it free for everyone.
Which files can I upload? ▼
JPG or PNG up to 25 MB. HEIC isn't supported yet. PNGs with transparency get a one-click “Convert to RGB and try again” helper for the cloud model.
Why did my first cloud photo take a few minutes? ▼
After a quiet period the AI model boots from cold — the first run can take a few minutes; the next ones finish in seconds.
